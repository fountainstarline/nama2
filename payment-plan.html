Our Payment Plans

We offer our clients different payment plans that suits their project budgets

Payment Plan A

You pay 40% upfront out of the 100% of your overall project cost and after three(3) months, you start paying in installment monthly until the total cost of the project is completed. Here the client enjoys seeing his App released and even launched before completion of the overall payment of the project.

Payment Plan B

You pay 30% upfront out of the 100% of your overall project cost and then after the first phase of the project is completed, you continue paying 20% on each new phase of the project completed, until the final phase of the project is reached and completed. Only then can the project be officially released to the client.

Payment Plan C

You pay 40% upfront out of 100% of your overall project cost before your project kicks off. Then within the middle of the project, another 30% is committed by the client. The rest of the payment is made by the client after the completion of the project. Only then can the project be officially released to the client.
